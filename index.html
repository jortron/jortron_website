About Me:

Born and raised in San Antonio, TX and moved to Austin in 2003 to play the drums. I've worked with a variety of musicians, bands, producers, major and independent record labels, designed and silkscreened t-shirts/tour posters/LP covers/cassettes, and toured North America and abroad many times.

After more than a decade of touring and playing music, I have turned my interests to graphic design and web development. As a recent graduate of the Web Development Immersive program from General Assembly, I discovered my love of front end development.

Projects:

My Thai Mom website was an exercise that involved picking a random technology and making something in one day. My partner and I chose the Middleman front end framework for static sites. We decided to design a much-needed website for the excellent food trailer, My Thai Mom. I enjoyed the simple site and thought it shows what I can accomplish with unfamiliar technology and a limited amount of time.
